## wind lamp

### Description

To light the lamp and illuminate a room.

### Syntax

	handVariable.wind{};

### Parameters

<dl>
  <dt>handVariable:</dt>
  <dd>Choose a hand.</dd>
</dl>

<dl>
  <dt>wind{}:</dt>
  <dd>Charge lamp to produce light.</dd>
</dl>
