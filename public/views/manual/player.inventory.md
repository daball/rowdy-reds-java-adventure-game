## inventory

### Description

Displays contents in right hand or left hand to the screen. 

### Syntax:

	System.out.println(handVariable);

### Parameters

<dl>
  <dt>System.out.println():</dt>
  <dd>Prints out contents.</dd>
</dl>


<dl>
  <dt>handVariable:</dt>
  <dd>Consist of left or right hand.</dd>
</dl>
