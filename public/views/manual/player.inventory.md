## inventory

### Description

Displays contents in hand to the screen. 

### Syntax:

	System.out.println{handVariable};

### Parameters

<dl>
  <dt>System.out.println{}:</dt>
  <dd>Prints out contents.</dd>
</dl>


<dl>
  <dt>handVariable:</dt>
  <dd>Choose a hand.</dd>
</dl>
