## crystalCloak

### Description

Add lamb chop to bowl, assuming you have a lamb chop in the hand you are referring too.

### Syntax:

	bowl = handVariable;

### Parameters

<dl>
  <dt>bowl:</dt>
  <dd>Consist of a game object.</dd>
</dl>

<dl>
  <dt>handVariable:</dt>
  <dd>Consist of left or right hand.</dd>
</dl>
