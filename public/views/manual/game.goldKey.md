## goldKey

### Description

Use certain keys to open locked doors.

### Syntax:

	handVariable = key;

### Parameters

<dl>
  <dt>handVariable:</dt>
  <dd>Consist of left or right hand.</dd>
</dl>

<dl>
  <dt>key:</dt>
  <dd>To open locked doors.</dd>
</dl>
