## tablet

### Description

Time to play a game.

### Syntax:

	me.equip{Tablet};

### Parameters

<dl>
  <dt>me:</dt>
  <dd>Consist of the player.</dd>
</dl>

<dl>
  <dt>equip{}:</dt>
  <dd>Assign player with some game items.</dd>
</dl>

<dl>
  <dt>tablet:</dt>
  <dd>Item equipped.</dd>
</dl>
