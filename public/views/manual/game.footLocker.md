## footLocker

### Description

Unlocks the foot locker, assuming you have the correct key in your hand.

### Syntax:

	footLocker.unlock(handVariable);

### Parameters

<dl>
  <dt>footLocker:</dt>
  <dd>Consist of a game object.</dd>
</dl>

<dl>
  <dt>unlock:</dt>
  <dd>Unlocks any door.</dd>
</dl>

<dl>
  <dt>handVariable:</dt>
  <dd>Consist of left or right hand.</dd>
</dl>
